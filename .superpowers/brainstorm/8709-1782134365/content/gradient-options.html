Premium Touches Across All Options

  • Glassy borders: 2px instead of 3px, subtle shadows
  • Glass morphism: subtle transparency overlays
  • Micro-interactions: smooth hover states (300-400ms)
  • Elevated typography: tighter tracking, better hierarchy
  • More whitespace: generous padding and margins
